Pannel Updates

Is your electrical panel outdated or struggling to keep up with your modern power demands? Upgrading your electrical panel is crucial for ensuring the safety, efficiency, and reliability of your home or business's electrical system. At Qayyum Electric, we specialize in professional electrical panel updates to meet your evolving needs.

Signs You Need an Electrical Panel Update:

Frequent Circuit Breaker Trips:
If your circuit breakers trip frequently, it could indicate that your electrical panel is overloaded and unable to handle the electrical load.

Outdated Panel Design:
Older electrical panels may have outdated designs or components that no longer meet modern safety standards. Upgrading to a newer panel can enhance safety and prevent potential hazards.

Insufficient Power Supply
If you're adding new appliances, electronics, or electrical fixtures to your home or business, your current electrical panel may not provide sufficient power. An update can ensure that your electrical system can handle your increased power needs.

Our Electrical Panel Update Services Include:

Panel Replacement:
We'll assess your current electrical panel and recommend the most suitable replacement option based on your power needs, budget, and safety requirements.

Upgrade to Circuit Breakers:
If your panel still uses fuses or outdated circuit breakers, we'll upgrade it to modern circuit breakers for improved safety and convenience.

Increase Amperage Capacity:
Upgrading to a higher amperage panel can accommodate increased power demands, allowing you to add more appliances and electronics without overloading the system.

Benefits of Updating Your Electrical Panel:

Improved Safety:

Improved Safety: Modern electrical panels are designed with advanced safety features to protect against electrical hazards such as shocks, fires, and overloads.

Enhanced Efficiency:

Upgrading to a more efficient electrical panel can reduce energy waste and lower your utility bills over time.

Increased Property Value:

A modern, up-to-date electrical panel adds value to your home or business and makes it more attractive to potential buyers.

Don't wait until electrical problems arise. Contact Qayyum Electric today to schedule an electrical panel update and ensure the safety and efficiency of your electrical system.
